Our Story

Founded in 2025 as a solo independent game studio, we started pursuing our game development dream from a small 5-square-meter room at home. Our goal has been to prove that inspiring, creative, and fun experiences can be delivered without large budgets.

PlayThyng creates experimental games that combine imagination with technology. If you'd like to learn more about us and our work, feel free to get in touch.
